Hello,

a bit about me :

I am a learning technology designer and researcher with over 10 years of experience in product design, child development, and educational technology. Entrepreneurial by nature with a keen sense of innovation, I have created over 100 games and toy products for young children, including numerous award winners and 4+ star reviews in the US and abroad. As an academic by training in the field of applied child development, I have a particular interest in the role of new media technologies in engaging children and families to promote social connection and positive youth development.
